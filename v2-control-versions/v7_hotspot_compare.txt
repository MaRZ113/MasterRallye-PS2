Master Rallye PS2 BX hotspot comparison v7
=========================================

Compared hotspots:
- hotspot_0x3C1004C0.bin
- hotspot_0x3100153C.bin
- hotspot_0x3700013E.bin

Key findings
------------
1. All three hotspots are pure BX regions: no <Scene, no <Egg.
2. hotspot_0x3100153C is a different BX subtype:
   - 0 BX*Hd markers
   - dominated by BX91|BXA0|BXI1 (61 clusters)
   - then BX91|BXA0 (46 clusters)
   - only 2 variant_chain entries
   This looks like an internal/secondary BX field, not a header-rich region.

3. hotspot_0x3C1004C0 is header-rich:
   - 18 BX*Hd markers
   - 13 primary_chain_no4
   - 2 full primary_chain
   - many mixed extensions (BXQ/BXU/BX{/BXT)
   Good candidate for recovering full BX header grammar.

4. hotspot_0x3700013E is the cleanest balanced region:
   - 12 BX*Hd markers
   - 6 full primary_chain
   - 4 primary_chain_no4
   - many stable BX91|BXA0|BXI1 repeats
   Best candidate for comparing header-rich and payload-like BX structures in one window.
