Master Rallye PS2 BX v9 notes
============================

Key findings from bx_chain_region_full.bin and bx_chain_region_variant.bin:

1. BX chains are real 16-record structures, not 4-record only.
2. The visible front tags (BX*Hd/BX91/BXA0/BXI1 or variants) are only the first quarter of the chain.
3. There are at least two stable BX families:
   - full-region dominant family: BX*Hd | BX91. | BXA0. | BXL..
   - variant-region dominant family: BX*Hd | BX91. | BXA0. | BXI .
4. In both families, rid 1..4 have very short payloads and look like a compact header stage.
5. rid 5..7 still look header-ish / control-like.
6. rid 8..15 are much larger and are likely the real payload-bearing stage.
7. rid 16 is terminal (no payload_len because it ends the chain).

Best next move:
- stop taking giant hotspot windows
- target SAME-FAMILY chain regions and compare several neighboring chains of the same family
